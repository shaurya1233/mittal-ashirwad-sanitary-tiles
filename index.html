Our Journey

The Ashirwad Story

◆

In 2005, Arvind Mittal opened a modest tiles showroom in Sarojini Nagar, Lucknow, with a vision that was simple but rare — to treat every customer like family, and every tile like an investment in someone's most personal space: their home.

Word spread — not through advertisements, but through the genuine experiences of families who had trusted Ashirwad with their most precious spaces. Each happy customer became an ambassador.

Today, Ashirwad stands as one of Lucknow's most trusted names in premium tiles and sanitaryware — carrying the finest brands, offering honest pricing, and maintaining the same personal touch that has defined us since day one.

Timeline

Our Milestones

2005

Founded by Arvind Mittal at Sarojini Nagar, Lucknow. A small showroom with a big dream of honest, quality business.

2008

Expanded tile collection to include Kajaria and Somany. Customer base grows rapidly through word-of-mouth.

2012

Added complete sanitaryware and bath fittings. Partnered with Jaquar and Hindware for premium offerings.

2015

Opened second location at LDA Colony, Power House Road — serving a wider community across Lucknow.

2017

GST Registration — formalizing our commitment to transparent, law-abiding, honest business practices.

Today

20+ years, 10,000+ happy customers, 50+ premium brands — and the very same promise as day one.
